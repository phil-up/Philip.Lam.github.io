New York City Crime Analysis

We analyzed the 2021 New York City (NYC) crime data obtained from the NYC OpenData website. Using Python, we cleaned the data and created visualizations in Tableau. Our analysis focused on identifying the most common crimes, crime spikes within each quarter of 2021, and the types of crimes committed in each borough. Based on our findings, we developed recommendations to help the NYPD allocate resources more effectively across the boroughs.
